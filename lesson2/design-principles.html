Israel Flores Design Principles

Rule of Thirds

Nest

nest.com

Nest does well with not putting everything in one place, You can see items are placed within the 2 by 2 grid. Especially products near the middle of the screenshot/page.

White Space and Clean Design

Ruckus Marketing

RuckusMarketing

This website utilizes great use in whitespace and clean design. There is plenty of room to breath and it doesn't make you feel, crowded. Its efficient when you hover your mouse some images and you can still see a clean design when the tile flips to the other side.

Hick's Law

Amazon

Amazon

Amazon follows the rule of Hick's Law pretty well, it keeps plenty of options for the user to view and at the same time it gives the user an easy amount of options and choices for the user to expand. It makes it easy to make a choice and narrow down the product the user wants.
